Odoo Meta WhatsApp POS

Connecting your business with WhatsApp is a boon for you!

This WhatsApp POS Module which allows the user to Create/Edit/Remove/Delete WhatsApp Numbers and Campaign, Schedule WhatsApp Messages, Showing WhatsApp Message History, Configure WhatsApp Templates in Particular WhatsApp Marketing Campaign via Odoo.
Let's have a look at Setup & Configuration!

Odoo Meta WhatsApp POS Features

  • Send WhatsApp Message & Order Receipt to Customer
  • Configure Different WhatsApp Templates to Customer

    • Setup & Configuration

    Install Odoo Meta WhatsApp POS Module

    From App Menu > Install Module

    Send by WhatsApp

    After installing the module, Go to Point of Sale > Create POS Order > After Validating POS Order > Click on Send by WhatsApp Button
    This will allow to Send WhatsApp Message & Particular Order Receipt to Customer.

    WhatsApp Composer

    After Click on Send by WhatsApp Button > WhatsApp Composer will Open
    Here you can Configure Different WhatsApp Messages & Templates to Customer
